Clinical Toxicology and Environmental Biomonitoring Laboratory





Our research focuses on the development of mass spectrometric techniques and their applications to:

  • Environmental biomonitoring
    •
  • Clinical toxicology
  • Therapeutic drug monitoring in HIV/AIDS patients.



Clinical Toxicology

Specializing in synthetic drug analysis the laboratory is actively involved in surveillance studies on designer drugs and the analysis of cases resulting from designer drug intoxication.

Environmental Biomonitoring

Pioneering the application of high resolution mass spectrometry to the screening of environmental chemicals in biological matrices.

HIV/AIDS and TB

The laboratory has developed a unique approach to the monitoring of therapeutic drugs in TB and HIV/AIDS patients which uses hair analysis to verify long-term medication compliance
